About us

What is electrify?

Electrify is a company started in 2010 and founded by Muthu Revathi V . It renders creative and innovative projects, ideas and guidelines in electronics to interested folks. Electrify has given a lot of successful projects to students, common people and also many companies.


Why Electrify?

Students of electronics field, looking for projects and ideas are welcome. This is the place where you can find successful and unique projects for your college programes.


To Whom is it for?

We are located in coimbatore are particularly for the engineering students or other stream college students or school students who are in need of final year projects or other projects in Electronics or other fields which relate to it. We provide various projects to the students in an affordable and reasonable price. Anyhow these are not only restricted for them, any people who wants the projects can approach us.


Is it useful for people other than students?

Yes! Electrify is proud to say that creative and useful projects are sold to common people to make their lives easier. Some of the projects that Electrify has established are specified in our You Tube channel as well as our gallery.
